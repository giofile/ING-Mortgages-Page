Welcome To ING Mortgages

A mortgage is a loan with a home as collateral.
Are you taking out a mortgage? Then one lender
(for example a bank) can advance you money for buying a home.
You will then give your home as collateral to the lender


With the National Mortgage Guarantee (NHG) you
have a safety net if circumstances prevent you from paying your mortgage.
Or if a residual debt remains after the sale of your house. The NHG
is a guarantee on mortgages for owner-occupied homes with a purchase
price up to the average house price
